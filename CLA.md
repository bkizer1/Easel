## How to sign

Adding a contribution counts as agreement to the above. For an explicit record,
sign off your commits with the [Developer Certificate of Origin](https://developercertificate.org/):

```
git commit -s -m "feat: your change"
```

which appends a `Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>` trailer.
